Purchasing HealthScope

Image taken from Easydigitaldownloads.com

To purchase HealthScope services, potential customers could access the sales website, where they could pay in cash or credit, or they could request their health provider to sign them up. Because we understand the value of our product and its potential urgency, we would implement payment plans so that customers who need to spread their payments out over a long period can have immediate access to our service. This will help us look out for customers' best interests and help ensure loyalty.

HealthScope would be advertised on tv and on relevant websites such as Web M.D to reach as many customers as possible. We understand that many people who could make use of our product will not have access to TV or the internet due to various potential disabilities and economic conditions. Therefore, we would partner with clinics and primary care providers to reach these customers via their doctors. We want as many people as possible to have the opportunity to purchase HealthScope and give themselves the peace of mind that they deserve.

If possible, we would make this available to purchase via insurance so that all people who need it can access HealthScope, not only those who can afford it outright. As our company grows, we will become better able to support philanthropic programs. For example, we may give a portion of each purchase to a fund that will allow us to provide free HealthScope services to the most vulnerable members of our community, such as the elderly or veterans.
